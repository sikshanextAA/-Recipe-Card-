Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A rich and flavorful classic Italian meat sauce served over spaghetti. Perfect for a comforting family dinner.

1 hr 15 min
4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 400g Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 Carrot, finely chopped
  • 400g Canned Crushed Tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 400g Spaghetti

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  2. Add chopped onion, garlic, and carrot. Cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low, cover, and let it cook for at least 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  4. About 15 minutes before the sauce is done, cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  5. Drain the spaghetti and serve hot with the bolognese sauce spooned over the top. Garnish with fresh basil or parmesan cheese if desired.
